Our Journey Together

My love,

They say the third year is when a relationship truly deepens—and though the distance keeps us apart, my heart has never felt closer to yours. We’ve weathered storms, celebrated victories, and built a love that defies every mile between us.

I love the way your laughter fills our calls, turning even my silliest jokes into something special. I love how you remember the little things, bringing me comfort from afar. I love our traditions—movie nights over screens, surprise deliveries, and counting down the days until I’m in your arms again.

Every day with you is an adventure, whether we’re exploring new cities together or sharing quiet moments through a screen. You make the ordinary extraordinary, and the distance only reminds me how fiercely I cherish you.
